Why This Data Matters

Key principles behind the analysis

High Conviction = Multiple Funds Agree

When 7 out of 10 professional fund managers independently hold the same stock, it signals deep research consensus and reduces single-point-of-failure risk. This is fundamentally different from retail hype.

Small Cap ≠ Small Company

MCX has a ₹62,000 Cr market cap. These are mid-sized, professionally run businesses with significant growth potential. "Small cap" refers to market cap classification, not business maturity.

Sector Diversification Built In

Banking, manufacturing, healthcare, tech — the top 10 naturally span multiple sectors. A basket of these stocks is more resilient than a concentrated bet on any single theme.

Exports Shield Against INR Volatility

4 of 10 stocks derive 50%+ revenue from exports (Apar, KEI, Tega, Kirloskar). Foreign currency earnings provide a natural hedge against rupee depreciation.

Institutional Buying = Price Support

When 5+ large funds hold a stock worth ₹15,000+ Cr AUM each, they provide a buying floor during market corrections. This reduces downside volatility for patient investors.

Track the Smart Money

These portfolios represent ₹1.5 Lakh Cr+ of professionally managed capital with full-time research teams, regulatory scrutiny, and fiduciary accountability. They align with serious investors.
